Fully executable scripts that implement published models used to simulate a set of empirical findings and/or illustrate principles of neural and/or psychological function.

  • FitzHugh Nagumo Model of Locus Coeruleus (Gilzenrat et al., 2002)
  • Dopamine and Temporal Differences Learning (Montague, Dayan & Sejnowski, 1996)
  • Locus Coeruleus and Attentional Blink (Nieuwenhuis, et al, 2005)
  • Proactive Control & Task Control: A Stroop Model (Kalanthroff et al., 2018)
  • Stroop GRAIN model (Cohen & Huston, 1994)
  • Conflict Monitoring and Cognitive Control (Botvinick et al., 2001)
  • LVOC Model of Stroop XOR Task (Bustamante et al. 2017)
